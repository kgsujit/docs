# Privacy Policy for Sudoku Trainer

**Last Updated:** [December 25, 2025]

## Introduction

Sudoku Trainer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

We use third-party services that may collect certain information automatically:

#### Firebase Analytics
- **App usage data**: How you interact with the app (screens viewed, features used)
- **Device information**: Device type, operating system version, app version
- **Performance data**: App crashes, errors, and performance metrics
- **Purpose**: To improve app functionality and user experience

#### Google AdMob
- **Advertising ID**: Used to serve personalized ads
- **Device information**: Device type, location (general), language
- **Ad interaction data**: Which ads are shown and clicked
- **Purpose**: To display relevant advertisements

**Note:** We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number directly.

### Information You Provide

- **Game preferences**: Difficulty settings, theme choices, font sizes
- **Game progress**: Puzzle completion data, statistics, scores
- **All data is stored locally** on your device and is not transmitted to our servers

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

1. **App Improvement**: Analyzing usage patterns to improve app features and performance
2. **Advertising**: Serving relevant ads through Google AdMob
3. **Analytics**: Understanding how users interact with the app to enhance user experience
4. **Crash Reporting**: Identifying and fixing bugs and crashes

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All game data (puzzles, progress, statistics) is stored **locally on your device**
- This data is **never transmitted to our servers**
- You can delete this data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

### Network Security
- All network communications use HTTPS encryption
- Firebase and AdMob connections are secured with TLS
- No sensitive data is transmitted in plain text

## Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

1. **Right to Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Right to Rectification**: Request correction of inaccurate data
3. **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your personal data
4. **Right to Restrict Processing**: Request limitation of data processing
5. **Right to Data Portability**: Request transfer of your data
6. **Right to Object**: Object to processing of your personal data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at Sujitkoduvalli@gmail.com.

## Data Deletion

### How to Delete Your Data

**Local Data (Game Progress, Settings):**
- Uninstall the app, or
- Clear app data from device settings

**Analytics Data:**
- Analytics data collected by Firebase is anonymized and aggregated
- Individual user data cannot be deleted from Firebase Analytics
- You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings

**AdMob Data:**
- You can reset your Advertising ID in your device settings
- This will prevent personalized ads but may not delete historical data

### Requesting Data Deletion

If you wish to request deletion of your data, please contact us at sujitkoduvalli@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

## Data Retention

- **Local game data**: Retained until you delete the app or clear app data
- **Analytics data**: Retained by Firebase according to their retention policies (typically 14 months)
- **Ad data**: Retained by AdMob according to their retention policies

## International Data Transfers

Data collected through Firebase Analytics and AdMob may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence. These transfers are made in accordance with applicable data protection laws and Google's privacy policies.
